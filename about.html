About Us

Established in the year of 2001 A.D. for providing integrated medical services through renowned doctors on OPD basis. The services available are Cardiology, General Medicine, Pediatric, Dermatology, ENT Surgeon, Gynecology, Psychiatric consultant, Orthopedic, Diabetes and Endocrino and etc. The other available facilities including clinical Laboratory, Digital X-ray, Echocardiography (Echo), Electrocardiogram (ECG) , Ultrasound (USG) , Holter Analysis, ABP monitoring, Hearing Evaluation, Adult Vaccination etc.

Vita Shield is available to patients of all ages , from infancy through adulthood. Physicians in our Internal Medicine Department see patients' age 15 and older,and care for many patients through their entire adulthood. They specialize in diagnosis and management of complex problems as well as long term care of patients with chronic disease and geriatric issues. They also provide preventative care to adults.Vita Shield's pediatricians specialize in disorders of babies, children and adolescents and are experts in preventive medicine and well childcare, as well as the care of ill children. Our medical staff is assisted by friendly and skilled pediatric nurse practitioners with advanced nursing training, who provide a broad range of healthcare services for infants, children and adolescents.
