#Our Department

DERMATOLOGY

VitaShield derma and laser clinic is run by experts to provide quality dermatological care of various conditions of skin, nail and hairs. This clinic is equipped with world class laser and light to provide affordable aesthetic services. Our lasers and light are designed for Asian skin for reduction of unwanted hair permanently, tattoo removal and photo rejuvenation. NB-UVB light is newer treatment modalities for psoriasis, vitiligo, eczema, itchy conditions and many more.

GYNECOLOGY & OBSTETRICS

This specialty of VitaShield deals with the females reproductive organs in pregnant and non-pregnant state. VitaShield supports women throughout their journey from childbearing age to menopause and beyond. The department of Gynecology provides preventive services and outpatient treatments for disorders of the female reproductive and urinary systems also treat women who have cancers of the female reproductive tract. Room devoted to labor, delivery and recovery provide a private setting to treat women with a range of needs. We provide minimal Invasive Surgery for most complex gynecological surgery.
